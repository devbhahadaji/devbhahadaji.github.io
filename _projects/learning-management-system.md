The Learning Management System (LMS) was developed to provide a complete e-learning solution for educational institutions and corporate training departments. Built with Laravel's robust framework, this platform delivers a seamless experience for both educators and learners.

## Technical Highlights

The architecture follows modern best practices with a clean separation of concerns, utilizing Laravel's MVC pattern. The SCORM integration required deep understanding of the standard specifications and careful implementation to ensure compatibility with various content authoring tools.

## Impact

This LMS has been deployed to serve thousands of users, facilitating online education with features that rival enterprise-level solutions while remaining cost-effective and easy to maintain.
