Hire And Fyre addresses the gap in the UAE construction equipment rental market by providing a digital platform that simplifies the rental process for both equipment owners and renters.

## Multi-Platform Strategy

The project involves simultaneous development of a React web application and a Flutter mobile app, sharing a common Laravel backend. This approach ensures feature parity across platforms while optimizing for each platform's strengths.

## Market Impact

Currently in active development, the platform is set to transform how construction companies in the UAE access heavy machinery, reducing idle equipment time for owners and improving equipment availability for contractors.
