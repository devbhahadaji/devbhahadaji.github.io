Rent-a-Lounge revolutionizes the way people book event spaces by providing a streamlined online platform. The eCommerce functionality includes sophisticated product variation management, allowing lounge owners to offer different packages and add-ons.

## Key Technical Features

The booking system implements complex availability logic, preventing double bookings while maximizing space utilization. The image management system includes a custom cropping tool for maintaining consistent gallery presentations.
